Available Commands

/setup [tag] [role] [interval]
Configure tag tracking with a 4-character tag, target role, and check interval in hours.
/remove [tag]
Remove a tracked tag and stop its automated role assignments.
/list
View all active tag trackers with their next check times and intervals.
/status
Check tagger statuses, view progress of running checks, and see detailed statistics.
/help
Get comprehensive help documentation and support server information.
